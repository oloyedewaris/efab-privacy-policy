We take your privacy seriously and are committed to safeguarding your personal information at Efab Properties (which term includes all subsidiaries of Efab Properties) (the “Company,” “we,” “us,” or “Efab Properties”). This policy describes the sources of your personal information that we may collect from you or that you may provide to us, our purposes for collecting and processing your information, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, retaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your personal information, please contact us using the details provided in the section “How do you contact us?”

3. . WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU AND HOW WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

Below is an explanation of the types of personal information we collect on or through our Platforms, why we collect it, and how we use it:

3.1. To Respond to Any Queries or Expressions of Interest Made by You

We use your personal data to help us respond to your queries or expressions of interest and ensure we help you find the right property. We may receive your personal data through one of the following channels:

  • When you send us an inquiry via our website, social media pages, phone, or WhatsApp regarding one of our properties.
  • When you participate in one of our events or visit our stands to inquire about our properties.
  • When you have expressed interest in our properties through our third-party agents.
  • When you visit our sales office or contact our sales advisors regarding any property you wish to purchase.

We collect the following personal information: name, email address, phone number, nationality, and details of your inquiry, pursuant to our legitimate business interests. In certain cases, your inquiry may relate to a specific service request (e.g., raising an inquiry about a specific property). For such requests, we need to use your personal information to respond to your request and fulfill our contractual obligations under the requested service.

Additionally, when you call our customer service center, we maintain a record of the communications, including call recordings, in pursuit of our legitimate business interest to monitor and improve the quality of our customer support.

3.2. To Enter Into or Conclude a Sales Purchase Agreement

Once you decide to purchase property with Efab Properties, we collect personal data such as name, passport or visa details, address, national ID, and contact information to finalize contractual arrangements for the sale and to facilitate registrations and payment arrangements. Additionally, to conclude sales, we are required to carry out a security clearance of our customers in compliance with local laws and pursuant to our legitimate business interest to prevent fraudulent transactions.

3.3. To Request Your Feedback and to Respond to Complaints

Your satisfaction is our top priority. To help us address feedback or complaints, we collect your name, email address, transaction details (such as the service you used), and your feedback/complaints—often through a survey conducted after a transaction or after a call with our customer service team. We collect and use this personal information to address your concerns and to continually improve our services, in pursuit of our legitimate business interests

3.4. To Personalize and Provide You with Information About Efab Properties’ Services and Latest Offers

We may combine the information we receive and collect about you to better understand your interests and preferences so that we can provide you with a tailored experience. For example, we may notify you of any events conducted by Efab Properties or send you personalized offers, discounts, or promotions via our mobile application or email (where you have agreed to receive such emails), or advertising content that is relevant to your interests. It is in our legitimate business interests to send you information about Efab Properties’ services and the latest offers. We may use your personal information, such as an email address or contact number, to share these communications.

3.5. To Facilitate Email Campaigns

As part of our email campaigns, we track whenever you receive, open, click a link, or download any attachment from an email you receive from Efab Properties. We may carry out automated profiling of such information to evaluate your interest in our service offerings or promotions. This helps us identify and target potential customers or business partners, tailor our marketing, and provide relevant and timely content based on your interests, in pursuit of our legitimate business interests.

3.6. To Facilitate Advertisements

Efab Properties uses the personal information you provide for commercial purposes to help tailor and display advertisements. We may partner with third parties to help us display relevant advertising and to manage our advertising across multiple channels, including social media platforms. Our third-party partners may use cookies and non-cookie-based technologies to help us show you advertising based on your browsing activities and interests.

3.7. To Establish a Working Relationship with Our Tenants, Third-Party Agents, and Business Partners

We collect or receive personal information about you through one of the following channels:

  • When you send us an inquiry for tenancy or if you were previously engaged in a tenancy arrangement with us.
  • When you participate in any of our sales, marketing, or thought leadership events (including webinars) organized or hosted by Efab Properties.
  • When our sales executives or business leaders attend events organized by external organizations (including the company you represent).
  • When our sales executives or business leaders share a professional/personal relationship with you.
  • From publicly available information on professional networking platforms such as LinkedIn

We collect personal information such as name, email address, contact number, job title, and the company you represent. We use this information in pursuit of our legitimate business interests to:

  • Share information relating to Efab Properties’ services and potential tenancy opportunities.
  • Invite you to future events that may be of interest to you or the company you represent.
  • Establish a working relationship with your organization.
3.8. To Enter Into, Renew, or Fulfill Service Contracts

We collect and use personal information such as name, email address, contact number, and in certain cases, passport/government ID details to facilitate the performance of a tenancy contract between Efab Properties and the organization you represent. We may also collect personal information (e.g., name, contact details, passport/government ID, etc.) of your organization’s employees who will be stationed in our malls/facilities for sales events, in order to facilitate security pass clearance. This processing occurs pursuant to our legitimate business interest and to fulfill our contractual obligations.

3.9 To Enter a Commercial or Investment Relationship

We collect and use information if you enter a commercial or investment relationship with us (e.g., as a shareholder or as a buyer of an asset in our portfolio). In line with contractual obligations, we may collect the following data directly from you, or from the company you work for or represent: name, contact details, phone number, postal address, email address, date and place of birth, percentage of capital and voting rights held, and any other data strictly related to the investment.

3.10. To Comply with Legal Requirements and Exercise or Defend Legal Claims

We may need to process and retain your personal information to comply with legal obligations (for example, in relation to licensing, health and safety). It is also in our legitimate interests to process personal information for exercising or defending legal claims. Processing personal information may be necessary to ensure compliance with relevant legal and regulatory obligations.

3.11. To Process Your Payments and Protect Against Fraudulent

We may process your personal information to keep your payment safe and secure and to protect against fraudulent transactions. It is in our legitimate interests to do so. We may also use your information to send payment reminders and legal notices in the case of overdue payments. This processing may be necessary for legal and regulatory compliance as well.

3.12. To Comply with Health and Safety Obligations

We may be required to process your medical or other health-related information in the event of an accident or injury at one of our residential units or communities. It is our legitimate interest to manage accidents and injuries occurring in our facilities and possibly to fulfill legal requirements or to prepare and defend against legal claims.

3.13. Other Information We Collect

In addition to the above, we may automatically collect certain information that does not directly identify you as an individual. Example:

  • Your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns are collected automatically as you navigate our websites.
  • Usage details, time of requests, browser type, operating system, IP addresses, and information collected through cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies.
  • Details of your visits to our website, including traffic data, location data, logs, and other communication data, and the resources you access or use on the website.

This information (often statistical data) helps us improve our website and services by:

  • Estimating our audience size and usage patterns.
  • Storing information about your preferences, allowing us to customize our website.
  • Speeding up your searches and recognizing you when you return to our site.

The technologies we use for automatic data collection may include:

  • Cookies: A small piece of data (letters and numbers) sent by a web browser and stored on your computer to remember information about you.
  • Flash Cookies: Local stored objects that collect and store information about your preferences and navigation to, from, and on our website.
  • Web Beacons: Small electronic files (e.g., clear gifs, pixel tags, single-pixel gifs) that permit the Company to track, for example, who has visited certain pages or opened an email.

We use these cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Identify you when you visit our platforms and help you navigate.
  • Help determine if you are logged into our platforms.
  • Store information about your preferences and personalize your experience.
  • Secure and protect your user account and our platforms.

6. HOW SECURE IS YOUR INFORMATION?

We prioritize the security of your personal data. We take measures to securely transmit personal information from your computer, smartphone, and other devices to our servers. We employ industry-standard security measures (e.g., firewalls, Transport Security Layer (TLS)) to keep your data confidential and secure.

Policies, and procedures designed to protect your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. Examples include:

  • Placing confidentiality requirements on our staff and service providers.
  • Restricting access to personal information strictly to those with a need to know (e.g., to respond to inquiries).
  • Destroying or anonymizing personal information when it is no longer needed.
  • Using secure communication channels for transmitting personal data.

The safety and security of your information also depend on you. Where we have provided a password (or you have chosen one) for access to parts of our Website, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. Please do not share it with anyone.

While we strive to protect your personal information, transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure. Any transmission of personal information is at your own risk, and we are not responsible for unauthorized access that circumvents any privacy settings or security measures on our Website.
